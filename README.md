# 🚗 Car Selling Price Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a car sales dataset to uncover meaningful business insights related to pricing, seller performance, vehicle condition, and yearly trends.

The goal of this project is to transform raw vehicle sales data into actionable insights using structured data analysis techniques.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze selling price trends across different years  
- Identify top-performing sellers in the marketplace  
- Understand the impact of vehicle condition on selling price  
- Determine the most frequently sold makes and models  
- Extract business insights that support pricing and sales strategies  

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Python  
- Pandas (Data Manipulation)  
- NumPy (Numerical Operations)  
- Matplotlib (Data Visualization)  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Seller Performance Analysis

Evaluated seller-wise sales distribution to identify top-performing sellers and their specialization in specific vehicle brands and models.

### 2️⃣ Make & Model Popularity

Identified the most sold vehicle make and model combinations across sellers to determine market demand patterns.

### 3️⃣ Price Distribution Analysis

Analyzed selling price variations to detect premium sales segments and overall market spread.

### 4️⃣ Year-wise Sales Comparison

Compared vehicle sales data across different years to observe trends, pricing shifts, and market behavior changes.

### 5️⃣ Condition Rating Impact

Studied how vehicle condition ratings influence maximum and average selling prices.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Vehicles with higher condition ratings consistently achieve better resale value.  
- Certain sellers specialize in specific brands, indicating niche market strategies.  
- A small segment of high-value vehicles significantly impacts total revenue distribution.  
- Market pricing shows noticeable fluctuations across years.  
- Some car models maintain strong resale demand regardless of year.
